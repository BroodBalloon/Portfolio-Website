About Me

I am a media designer who is currently studying media design & ICT at Fontys.

Qualities

What I Do.

I create round concepts that speak to a target audience and fills their needs. This concept is excecuted and tested with the target audience. In the end I deliver a product that is easy to use and clear for the target audience.

User experience

When I create something I test this with the end user. I improve the end product by itteration. Every Itteration is tested with the user. In the end you get a product that users can use without any problems or irritation.

Concepting

With concepting I can help you get a product that fits your target audience. This is accomplished by inspecting the target audience and finding out their needs. You take these needs and find a solution for them. Then the solution is branded in a way that it is appealing to the target audience. There can be different media products depending on the target audiance.

Storytelling

Storytelling is needed to get a message across to a target audience. I can take a message and build a story around it that is understandable for the target audience.

Web Design

I use Webdesign to speak to a target audience. Webdesign can be anything on the web, from social media to a website. Before you design something on the web you need a good concept that speaks to a target audience.
