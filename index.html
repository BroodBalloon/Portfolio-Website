About Me

I am an User Experience Designer who is currently concepting and designing OCC Mobile at Eurocom Group BV

Qualities

What I do

I create round concepts that speak to a userbase and fulfill their needs. The concept is executed and tested with the target audience. In the end I deliver a product that is easy to use and intelligible for the customer.

User experience

When I create something I test this with the end user. I improve the product by itteration and every reiteration is tested with the user. Eventually you get a product that users can use without problems or annoyances.

Concepting

Within the proces of concepting I can bring forth a product that meets the personal needs of the client. This is accomplished by inspecting the target audience and finding out their necessities. By analysing the issues I compose multiple concepts and check if this solves the complications. The outcome can be surprising.

Storytelling

Storytelling is getting your message across in a way that connects to stakeholders and a target audience. I can translate the essense of a product by presenting it in a way that is comprehensible for everyone.

App & Web Design

The Front-End of the final product is essential for the user experience. Without this a fantastic product can be percieved as inadequate. To resolve this I make sure that for the final product the usage aswell as the look and feel are in unison.
